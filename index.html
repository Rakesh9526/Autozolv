A Legacy of Quality and Innovation

With decades of experience in the construction, automotive, and hygiene industries, AL SAFI GLOBAL MANUFACTURING INDUSTRY LLC is a name synonymous with quality and dependability. Every member of the management team works hard to provide clients with technical guidance, high-quality products, and round-the-clock assistance. This dedication has led to success stories that define our legacy since inception. AL SAFI Global Manufacturing Industries produces building products, bituminous waterproofing products, automotive products, and cleaning chemicals marketed under the ZOLVTEK, AUTOZOLV, and HYGIEE brands. The superior research and control procedures used during pre-, production, and post-production phases are the secret to our success.

Autozolv: Pioneering Clean Solutions for Diesel Emissions

AUTOZOLV, is a trusted and renowned brand in dealing with harmful emissions from diesel vehicle exhaust. As a leading manufacturer of Diesel Exhaust Fluid (DEF)also known as AdBlue, Autozolv is dedicated to helping the transportation industry by reducing diesel emissions and contributing to a cleaner, healthier environment. Founded on the principles of innovation and environmental responsibility, Autozolv combines cutting-edge technology with rigorous quality standards to produce high-grade AdBlue. Autozolv products are designed to optimize the performance of diesel engines, ensuring compliance with stringent emission regulations while enhancing fuel efficiency
