About Us

A Legacy of Quality and Innovation

With decades of experience in the construction, automotive, and hygiene industries, AL SAFI GLOBAL MANUFACTURING INDUSTRY LLC is a name synonymous with quality and dependability. Every member of the management team works hard to provide clients with technical guidance, high-quality products, and round-the-clock assistance. This dedication has led to success stories that define our legacy since inception.

AL SAFI Global Manufacturing Industries produces building products, bituminous waterproofing products, automotive products, and cleaning chemicals marketed under the ZOLVTEK, AUTOZOLV, and HYGIEE brands. The superior research and control procedures used during pre-, production, and post-production phases are the secret to our success.

Our Mission

Our goal is to inspire widespread adoption of our superior products and services while addressing challenges faced by the industry. We aim to take our brand global and ensure customer satisfaction worldwide.

Our Vision

To expand our presence into new nations and establish OUR BRAND as the premier global supplier of solutions for construction, automotive, and hygiene industries.

Our Core Values

Quality and trust serve as the cornerstones of our business philosophy. We are dedicated to upholding the highest standards in product quality, safety, and reliability. Trust is not merely a word to us; it is a commitment that we honour in every transaction, interaction, and partnership.

  • Quality: Ensuring premium standards in every product.
  • Innovation: Continuously evolving to meet environmental needs.
  • Sustainability: Committed to eco-friendly practices.
  • Customer Satisfaction: Prioritizing our customers’ success.
  • Integrity: Building trust with transparency.
