Diesel Exhaust Fluid (DEF)
Diesel exhaust fluid (DEF) used in vehicles with Selective Catalytic Reduction (SCR) technology to reduce harmful gases being released into the atmosphere. DEF is a 32.5 % solution of high-purity, synthetically manufactured urea in de-mineralized water, in compliance with ISO 22241. It is safe-to-use fluid. DEF is categorized by the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) as safe for use since it is non-toxic, non-flammable, non-hazardous, non-polluting, and biodegradable.
DEF
Diesel Exhaust Fluid (DEF) is a solution of water and urea that's injected into diesel vehicles to reduce harmful emissions.
AUS 40%
AUS 40 is a 40% aqueous urea solution (AUS) that is used as a Diesel Exhaust Fluid (DEF). It is also known as Marine DEF.
AUS 50%
Diesel Exhaust Fluid (DEF) AUS 50% is a liquid solution of urea and water that's used to reduce nitrogen oxide emissions from diesel engines.
