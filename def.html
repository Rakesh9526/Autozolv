What is Diesel Exhaust Fluid?
Addressing the global issue of smoke emissions, SCR (Selective Catalytic Reduction) technology is essential for diesel vehicles. Our solution, AUTOZOLV DEF, leads the way with innovation and environmental responsibility. This advanced fluid is free of harmful substances and unpleasant odors, drastically reducing emissions and protecting your engine.

What is DEF?

AdBlue DEF (Diesel Exhaust Fluid) is a high-purity urea solution designed to reduce harmful emissions from diesel engines. It is injected into the exhaust system to break down nitrogen oxides (NOx) into harmless nitrogen and water vapor. AdBlue is a critical component in meeting environmental standards for diesel-powered vehicles and industrial machinery.

It is a non-toxic, colorless, and odorless fluid that ensures efficient operation of SCR (Selective Catalytic Reduction) systems, providing cleaner air by significantly lowering NOx emissions and improving overall engine performance.

How does AUTOZOLV DEF fluid work?

When diesel engines burn fuel, polluting chemicals and compounds are produced, including nitrogen oxides and nitrogen dioxide. These elements accumulate in the air and form smog and acid rain.

Quality Control

AUTOZOLV DEF is manufactured in accordance with ISO 22241, AUS 32, and DIN 70070 specifications. These recommendations describe the required manufacturing, testing, and handling techniques to ensure the best quality diesel exhaust fluid.

Monitoring

We provide complete tank monitoring services to ensure that you never run out of diesel exhaust fluid. Our cutting-edge monitoring systems maintain track of your fluid levels in real-time, alerting you when supplies are low.
