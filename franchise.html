Benefits & Applications

Benefits

  • Clear liquid that is non-toxic, non-polluting, and non-hazardous
  • Reduces harmful pollution
  • Eco-friendly
  • Improves air quality
  • Safe to handle
  • Easy to store
  • Improves vehicle compliance with pollution requirements
  • DEF can increase your car’s miles per gallon
  • DEF can enhance the engine performance

Applications

  • Commercial vehicles: Trucks, refuse trucks, fire engines, and other commercial vehicles with selective catalytic reduction (SCR) technology
  • Light-goods vehicles: Vans and other light-goods vehicles with SCR technology
  • Passenger vehicles: SUVs, pickups, camper vans, cars, coaches, buses, school buses, and minibuses fitted with SCR technology
