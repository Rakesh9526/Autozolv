Autozolv: Purity for Peak Battery Performance
Battery water, also known as battery fluid, is highly purified water used to maintain the electrolyte level in lead-acid batteries. It's essential for the performance and longevity of batteries in a variety of applications, including automotive, industrial, and stationary batteries.Autozolv Battery Water, also known as battery fluid, is a highly purified water specially designed to maintain the electrolyte levels in lead-acid batteries. Its exceptional purity ensures that no minerals or impurities compromise the chemical integrity of the battery, making it essential for optimal performance and extended battery life.


The electrolyte in your battery is a mixture of sulfuric acid and water. Battery water, on the other hand, is the clean water used to refill the electrolyte when its levels run low. The water used in battery water is usually distilled water or deionized water. It's never tap water, as tap water may contain impurities.

Applications

Primarily used in lead-acid batteries, Battery Water is essential for automotive, industrial, and stationary batteries, where maintaining the right electrolyte level is critical.

Quality and Purity

Battery Water is characterized by its extreme purity, being devoid of minerals and contaminants that could degrade battery performance.

Handling and Storage

Store in a cool, dry location away from direct sunlight.
